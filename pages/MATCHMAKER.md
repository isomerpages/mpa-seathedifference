# A Journey with the Matchmaker

##### Individuals at a career crossroad gets referred to visit a unconventional matchmaking agency that helps them to find ‘the one’ via the ideal career path. They are skeptical when the enigmatic matchmaker points them to an industry that isn’t appealing them. Fortunately, the matchmaker has an unconventional solution and brings them on a journey to see the difference.
